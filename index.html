About Us

PRAYUDDHA'2K23

We the students of Information Technology conducting Prayuddha, a national level symposium. Prayuddha is the word derived from the great literature Mahabharatham. It means War or Fight. Here it is depicted as a fight or technical challenge between rivals. We are organizing this symposium to invite and involve the students from various regions to compete with others and get to know about various trending technologies.

"Talent you have naturally, Skill is only developed by hours and hours and hours of beating your own craft" - Will smith

With respect to the above quote - Come up with your talents, train your skills on both tech and non-tech events and keep on your spirit. We welcome you all to the most prestigious occasion Prayuddha'23. Glad to have you on board.
